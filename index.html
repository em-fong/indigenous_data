Assignment #5: Native/Indigenous Communities in the United States

Original Dataset

This dataset comes from the 2010 US Census, and is a collection of information about the various land holdings of Native/Indigenous and Hawaiian groups in the country. The data is organized by geographic identifier code, American National Standards code, tribe/group name, population count, housing unit count, area of land holdings in square meters, area of water in square meters, land in square miles, water in square miles, and latitude/longitude coordinates.
